## Champion archer

_Medium humanoid (any race), any alignment_

**Armor Class** 17 (studded leather)

**Hit Points** 121 (22d8+22)

**Speed** 30ft.

**STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA**

12 (+1) 20 (+5) 12 (+1) 12 (+1) 16 (+3) 12 (+1)

**Saving Throws** Str +5, Con +5, Wis +7

**Skills** Athletics +5, Intimidation +5, Perception +7, Acrobatics +9

**Senses** passive Perception 17

**Languages** any one language (usually Common)

**Challenge** 9 (5,000 XP)

**Indomitable (2/LR).** The champion archer rerolls a failed saving throw.

**Archer’s Eye (1/Turn).** As a bonus action, the champion archer can add 1d12 to its next attack or damage roll with a longbow or shortbow.

### Actions

**Multiattack.** The champion archer makes three attacks.

**Shortsword.** Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d6+5) piercing damage, plus 7 (2d6) piercing damage if the champion archer has more than half of its total hit points remaining.

**Longbow.** Ranged Weapon Attack: +11 to hit, range 600 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (1d8+5) piercing damage, plus 7 (2d6) piercing damage if the champion archer has more than half of its total hit points remaining.
